## üìñ Manifesto

**This is not a simple tool repository, but a "Laplace's Demon" fighting against information entropy.**

Penetration testing is fundamentally an information game. However, we face:
1.  **High Entropy of Tools**: Scripts scattered across GitHub, complex configurations, and incompatibility.
2.  **High Entropy of Thinking**: Methodologies stuck in attackers' minds, lacking standardized Prompts and SOPs.
3.  **High Entropy of Knowledge**: Quality results drowned in fragmented articles and blogs.

This repository is dedicated to **maintaining "Negentropy" in these three directions**: collecting, cleaning, and structuring them.
